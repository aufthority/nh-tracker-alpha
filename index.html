How You're Graded

How You're Graded

Your Hero Score is the average of all four category percentages. Foundational habits (⭐⭐⭐) gate the gauge — complete at least one to unlock above 50%.

S
100% — Perfect DayAll four neurochemicals fully activated.
A
80–99% — ExcellentStrong across the board. All foundational habits done.
B
60–79% — GoodFoundational habits complete in most categories.
C
40–59% — OkaySome habits done but foundational gaps remain.
D
20–39% — LowMinimal habits completed. Pick one category and start.
—
0–19% — Not StartedA new day waiting to be shaped.

Scoring Rules

Raw % = (completed habit weights ÷ total possible) × 100.

Foundational Gate: Any single ⭐⭐⭐ habit unlocks the full gauge. Until then, gauge is capped at 50%.

Serotonin exception: Has 3 foundational habits — completing ANY ONE of them unlocks the full gauge.

Flex Mode: Long-press or tap Flex to reduce a habit's weight on harder days.

Hero Score = average of all four effective category percentages.
